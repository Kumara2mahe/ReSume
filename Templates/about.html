About US

We can help you build your resume with our awesome templates

Re-Sume’s resume builder allows you to make as many changes to your design as needed. You can tweak your experience for any job within minutes. Our resume maker also allows you to make copies of your original design and save as many versions as you need.

Whether you’re applying for a role as a creative director, an accountant or your first internship, Re-Sume’s free resume maker has taken the heavy-lifting out of resume design—so you can focus on getting hired. Simply choose from hundreds of designer-made templates, highlight your skills and previous experience, then send it to employers with confidence.

To help give your recruiter a better sense of who you are, Re-Sume’s resume templates are completely customizable. Change the font, colors, layout and images with our easy, drag-and-drop tool. Created for the non-designer, Re-Sume’s resume creator is built to make designing your resume fast and easy.
